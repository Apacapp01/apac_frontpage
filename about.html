Introducing APAC – AI-Powered Accessibility Chatbot.

We trained a Chatbot That Responds to Sign Language! APAC is an innovative chatbot designed to make communication more accessible for everyone. Using advanced AI, APAC can understand sign language gestures through video input, providing seamless interaction in real-time. Whether you prefer using sign language, text or commands.APAC ensures that everyone can communicate effortlessly.

We are excited to bring this revolutionary technology to you and would love to hear your feedback as we continue to improve APAC. Try it now and experience the future of accessible communication!

With our own dataset and training, we got an 80% prediction rate!
